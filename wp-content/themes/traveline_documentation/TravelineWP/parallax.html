Parallas Home Page Setup

  1. Go to Pages > Add New
  2. Add your Page Title in the Title field
  3. Select Parallax page Template from the Template selection on the right side of the page
  4. Select Some Shortcode from FromShortcode for Homepage
  5. Edit Necessary text From your choosen shortcode
  6. Click Publish
  7. Parallax page banner option comes from theme optios
