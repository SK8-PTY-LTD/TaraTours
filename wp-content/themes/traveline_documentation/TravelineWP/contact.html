Contact Page Setup

  1. Go to Pages > Add New
  2. Add your Page Title in the Title field
  3. Select Default Template from the Template selection on the right side of the page
  4. Select Shortcode > Contact FromShortcode
  5. Edit Necessary text From All contact Shortcode
  6. Click Publish

3.)How to Get Contact Form

  1. Download and activate this plugin: http://wordpress.org/extend/plugins/contact-form-7/
  2. Go to Contact > Contact Form > Add New and paste the bellow code in the Form field and you get a shortcode
  3. Copy that shortcode and past in the contact Shortcode Field in Contact Us Shortcode
<div>[text* text-679 akismet:author placeholder "Your Name"][text* email-980 placeholder "Email Address"][text* text-885 placeholder "Subject"]</div>
<div>[textarea* textarea-223 30x10 placeholder "Message..."]</div>[submit class:button "Submit Now"]
